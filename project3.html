ARCHITECTURE • 2017-2021

Designing for Spatial Literacy

Background

Before transitioning to EdTech, I worked as an architectural designer directing technical planning for 12 public buildings. My focus was not just on structure, but on how users navigate and understand space.

Design Strategy

I utilized BIM (Building Information Modeling) and visual cognition theories to solve navigation problems. By implementing a color-coded zoning system, I created an environment that supported "Spatial Literacy," helping students intuitively understand their location.

Reflection

This project was pivotal in my realization that designing physical scaffolding for bodies is remarkably similar to designing cognitive scaffolding for minds.

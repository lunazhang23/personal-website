PRACTICE • 2025

From Passive to Constructive Inquiry

Project Scope

In my role as an educator, I designed a comprehensive 2-day training program for new hires. The goal was to shift the teaching paradigm from simple knowledge transmission to fostering active student inquiry.

Design & Implementation

Based on the ICAP theory, I developed training modules that demonstrated how to scaffold student questions to promote deeper cognitive engagement.

Outcome

The training program successfully equipped new teachers with the strategies to implement inquiry-based learning. Feedback indicated a significant shift in instructional approach, with participants demonstrating increased confidence in scaffolding student questions and fostering a more active, constructive classroom environment.
